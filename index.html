About Me

Hi there! My name is Rinki and I am from Bihar, India. I enjoy meeting new people and making friends. I always like to Build Castle in the Sky. I love Dreaming with an Open Eyes, Always Looking for a Something interesting in this universe. My hobbies include Watching movies and listening to soothing music while working, this makes me relax you know!
I am a kind of person who enjoys every second of her life to the fullest. I had not planned much about my future because experience had shown me that didn't work. Presently I am a B.com graduate, working women living a happy life with my nuclear family. I love my self, my family, my country and passionate about promoting independence and serving the aging population in my community.
